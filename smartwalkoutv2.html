Smart Walkout – Quick Guide

1. Project Info

  • Enter your project name and technician name for record-keeping.

2. Add Stations

  • Record Current GPS – enter a location name and tap to capture your current position as a station.
  • Edit a station's name and coordinates directly in the inventory table (Type = ●).

3. Connect Stations

  • Use the canvas to connect stations: tap one, then another.
  • Enter an optional connection name and confirm.
  • The line (Type = ━) shows the approximate distance in feet.

4. Track Polylines

  • Tap Start Track to begin recording your path.
  • A vertex is recorded at your location when tracking begins, and then each time you move ~0.00001° from the last vertex.
  • Tap Stop Track to stop and add a final vertex at your current location.
  • The resulting polyline (Type = ≋) appears in the inventory table and on the canvas with total distance in feet.

5. Quick Capture Buttons

  • Define custom buttons in bulk by entering labels (one per line or comma-separated).
  • Tap any button to capture a GPS point with that button label.
  • All captured points are added to the inventory.

6. View & Edit Inventory

  • The inventory table shows stations, lines, tracks, their names, endpoints/info, and distance.
  • You can rename or delete any record from this list.

7. Auto-Save & Export

  • Changes are auto-saved to your browser after a short delay.
  • Export to KML to view stations and lines in Google Earth Pro.
  • Export to CSV for spreadsheet analysis.

8. Theme & Clear Session

  • Use Toggle Light/Dark to switch themes (preference is saved).
  • Use Clear Session to wipe all data and start fresh on this device.
