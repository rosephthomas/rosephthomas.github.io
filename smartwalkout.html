Smart Walkout – Quick Guide

1. Add Points

  • Record Current GPS – enter a name and tap to capture your current position.
  • Edit a point’s name and coordinates directly in the table.

2. Connect Points

  • Use the canvas to connect points: tap one, then another.
  • Enter an optional connection name and confirm.
  • The line shows the approximate distance in feet.

3. Track Polylines

  • Tap Start Track to begin recording your path.
  • A vertex is recorded at your location when tracking begins, and then each time you move ~0.0001° from the last vertex.
  • Tap Stop Track to stop and add a final vertex at your current location.
  • The resulting polyline appears in the Links table and on the canvas with total distance in feet.

4. View & Edit Links

  • The Links table shows connections and tracks, their names, endpoints/type, and distance.
  • You can rename or delete any link or track from this list.

5. Auto-Save & Export

  • Changes are auto-saved to your browser after a short delay.
  • Export to KML to view stations and lines (including polylines) in Google Earth Pro.

6. Clear Session

  • Use Clear Session to wipe all data and start fresh on this device.
